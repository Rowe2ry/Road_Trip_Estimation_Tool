Frequently Asked Questions


Q: How does Getaway adjust for speed or traffic patterns?

A: Getaway calculations are based on an average speed of 60 miles per hour. Currently, traffic patterns are not factored. Speed and traffic patterns are scheduled to be included in the next Getaway update.


Q: We have several drivers on this trip. We want to drive straight, with only gas stops. Can Getaway factor in our 17 hours-no stop preference?

A: Getaway values safe driving practices, therefore our calculations are based on 15-minute restroom breaks every 2 hours and a maximum drive time of 10 hours per day. For trips requiring more than 10 hours of drive time, a hotel stay will be scheduled. A 30 minute stop for lunch and 1 hour stop for dinner is also included in the travel time.


Q: I drive a Model S Tesla, can Getaway account for my charging time?

A: Getaway Version 1.0 does not support charging time for electric cars. Fuel stops are based on a driving range of 300 miles and the assumption that vehicle starts with a full tank of gas.

Input parameters for gas mileage and vehicle type will be included in Getaway Version 2.0
